About Me

I grew up in Kerela, India and I started having an interest in computers at the age of 8 years old. My sister and I would mess around with the machine our family had and played the classic mine game or solitaire. It wasn't until I was in my freshman/sophmore year of highschool that I was exposed to programming. I took an HTML course that taught me how to manipulate websites. This allowed me to impress my friends by being able to swag out my Facebook, page. Since then, I have continued my interest in the web development and the infinite possibilities that it posseses.

I am now 27 years old and I live in Philadelphia, PA. My interests have changed but that fire to manipulate the virtual world still keeps me pushing to create something great. For fun I do weighlifting, learning to play guitar, and basketball. I love to spend time with my family, my friends, my dog, Jupiter and my lifting buddies. My ultimate goal is to create a technology that makes this world better than we found it.
